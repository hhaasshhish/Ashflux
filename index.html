About Me

Hello! I'm Aashish Singh Thakur

My professional journey through mechanical contracting and creative technology has ignited a deep curiosity within me—not just about physical machines, but about the intricate mechanics of our world: the systems of society, the evolution of technology, and the complexities of human behaviour.

This website stems from that curiosity. It's a space where I explore and share observations on diverse human experiences – our thoughts, dreams, and collective impact on the global stage. I delve into the dynamics of failure and success, wealth and poverty, and how technology shapes our lives across generations.

Here, I aim to scientifically dissect complex issues, understand the driving forces behind societal trends, analyze prevailing ideologies, and explore how we can navigate our world more effectively. Join me in this exploration of growth, economics, social psychology, and the fundamental forces shaping our shared future.
